How to avoid pinworm infection at home

Good hygiene is the best way to prevent pinworm

  • Thoroughly and frequently washing hands, including under the fingernails, with soap and warm water is important
  • Keep fingernails short. Avoid fingernail-biting and putting fingers in the mouth or on the face

If you see a child scratching his or her bottom, be sure that child washes their hands thoroughly, right away.



Other ways to avoid pinworm infection include:

Children may pick up infective pinworm eggs at school and bring the infection home.
  • Daily morning showers and changing of underwear
  • Carefully handle and frequently wash underwear, bed clothes, towels, and bedding
