Patient Assistance Program

To be eligible to receive prescription medication from Amneal, patients must

  • Live in one of the 50 U.S. states, District of Columbia, Puerto Rico or the U.S. Virgin Islands,
  • Meet applicable age restrictions for the medication
  • Meet certain total household income eligibility requirements
  • Have no affordable coverage for the medication (be uninsured or functionally uninsured)
  • Have an out-of-pocket cost greater than $200 for the medication, if enrolled in a Medicare Part D program
