Resources designed to help you

Your doctor has prescribed EMVERM to treat an infection caused by pinworms, highly contagious parasitic intestinal worms. Follow your doctor’s instructions carefully.

In addition to your doctor’s treatment, you can help prevent reinfection and infection of other people by understanding a few simple facts about pinworm.

Download the EMVERM Patient Brochure to learn more about pinworm infection, taking EMVERM, and how to save on your EMVERM prescription.
